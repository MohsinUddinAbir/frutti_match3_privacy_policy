Frutti Match3 Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile game *Frutti Match3* ("the Game") developed by Mohsin Uddin.

1. Data Collection

The Game does not collect any personal data from its users. We do not ask for, collect, or store any personally identifiable information, such as your name, email address, phone number, location, or device identifiers.

2. Permissions

The Game does not require any special permissions to run on your device. It only uses the standard functionalities necessary for a basic gaming experience.

3. Third-Party Services

The Game does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or social media integration services. No user data is ever sent to or shared with third parties.

4. Gameplay Data

The Game may store game progress locally on your device to allow you to resume your game and track your high scores. This data is not sent off your device and is not accessible to us.

5. Children's Privacy

The Game is a general audience game and does not knowingly collect any data from children. As we do not collect any personal data from any user, this game is in compliance with regulations regarding children's privacy.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. This policy is effective as of 8/28/2025.
